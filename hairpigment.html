Hair Pigmentation Treatment

Today hair loss is a significant problem among men and women. Hair loss can have intense effects on both men and women, lowering self-esteem and self-confidence. As it is called a King is worthless without his crown, so hair is your crown, and losing it can emotionally traumatize you. Is your crown falling apart? Come and get it back.

We at cosset world offer an array of beauty and cosmetic services that will surely change your lifestyle. Our service of Hair Pigmentation is notably prolific, adding stars to your look. We create such an underlying effect, which covers up the baldness and gives an appearance of a natural, full head hair. Scalp Pigmentation is perfect for hair thinning in areas around the crown portion of the head. If your scalp is visible through your hairs, SMP is capable of hiding them. This method is widely used and has zero side effect regardless of skin and hair type.

Benefits of Hair Pigmentation

Hair Pigmentation or Scalp Micropigmentation Micropigmentation (SMP) is an advanced method of cosmetic surgery (tattooing precisely). Being similar to traditional tattooing, it uses unique ways to cover your hair loss. This method is highly effective for bald people who want to keep a cropped head with a stubble look. Basically, in this process, the areas where the hair is thinning is targeted and tattooed with special ink and specially designed tiny needles. The ink selected after examining the scalp and the hair colour, for the best possible result. This method is also applicable for hiding scars left over from a surgery or a wound. This treatment is quick, and results can be visible from the first visit. SMP is a totally drug-free, scar less and non-surgical treatment.

Some of the benefits of SMP mentioned below-

Risk-Free

The topmost benefit of SMP is that it is totally free of risks like infections, incision. There is no pain during the process due to the use of local anaesthesia. You don't have to wait for the grafts to evolve and grow.

Cost-Effective

Unlike hair transplant, scalp micropigmentation cost is drastically low. When comparing both treatments, scalp micropigmentation costs 1/3 less than a surgical hair transplant. With scalp micropigmentation, there is no need for any kind of medication

Faster Healing Time

Scalp micropigmentation grants you a swifter healing process, usually from 2-4 days. You can easily return to your daily routine within two days. You will only need to revisit for touch-ups approximately every 12 months. The important thing to note is that colour can be altered according to clients need and requirements.
