PRP Hair Treatment

PRP- Regrowth of hairs with non-interfering technique

Hair loss has become a common problem all across the world, both men and women are under the same hood, although reasons may vary. Some acquire this due to bad eating, sleeping or physical habits others have genetically acquired. But taking measures on time can help you sustain those remaining hairs and even regrow new hairs naturally. Those people who have already reached a stage of baldness should go for hair transplantation. But if your hair is falling at an alarming rate, still you have a pretty decent amount of them to save you should definitely go for PRP Hair Treatment.

What is Platelet Rich Plasma (PRP) Treatment?

Platelet Rich Plasma therapy is a non-operational treatment where your platelet-rich plasma from your own blood is extracted and then injected into the scalp. This process stimulates the growth factor in your blood cells to do their job and grow hairs naturally. The platelets are the clotting cells in our blood which have the potential of accelerated healing. PRP for hair hair is the perfect treatment for both men and women. Dermatologists recommend at least 4-6 concourses with an interim of approx. 4-5 weeks between each concourse for obtaining the best results.

What is the process of PRP?

Mesenchymal stem cells contained in the human blood have growth factors, that help in the development of various aspects of the body. These cells are extracted, treated and then injected in the scalp of the targeted areas. The complete process mentioned below-

Sampling of blood

Platelets separation

Growth factor & Concentration of plasma

Injecting PRP
